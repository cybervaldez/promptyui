Recommendation Summary

Best overall: Phosphor (Light weight) — has hand-tap, cursor-click, hand-swipe, plus 6 weight variants for fine-tuning thickness.
Runner-up: Lucide — cleanest SVGs, tree-shakeable, but no tap icon.
kbd.css note: It's a tiny CSS-only lib for 3D keycap styling. The "3D Keycap" approach above replicates it. No need to import separately.
For kbd styling: The 3D Keycap or Minimal Outline approaches work best on dark backgrounds.
