Ctrl+Enter Block Picker — 5 Variations

Each variation shows the same picker concept: press Ctrl+Enter to choose between New Line, Nest Child, or Fork. The last choice is sticky (shown with a dot or highlight). Click options to change the sticky default.

Variation 1 — Horizontal Pill Bar
Three pills in a row below the editing block. The sticky default is filled solid, others are outlined. Replaces the hint area. Click a pill to set sticky default.
